About the Spaces & This Website

Designed for student societies: easier room info, smoother booking

What can you do here?

  • Browse room details: layout, capacity, equipment, and availability
  • Check free time slots, Exec members can book directly
  • Manage your bookings in “My Booking” (reschedule / cancel / rebook)
  • Read policies for entry, cancellation, and usage

How it works

  1. Select a building and room → check available slots
  2. Login (or register) → Exec members can confirm booking
  3. Receive confirmation → follow guidance to enter on the day
